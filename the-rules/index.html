This sections is about the rules of rugby. Like any game there are a lot of rules so we will go over the top 10 rules to get you going and then there will be a video at the end to make it that much easier....Good luck.


  1. There are 15 people on each side.
  2. To score points you have to put the ball down on the other end of the pitch for 5 pts and kick for another 2pts
  3. The ball must always be passed backwards
  4. If you drop the ball or knock it on the other team get possesion in a scrum
  5. You must tackle people below the shoulder.
  6. You cannot tackle people in the air.
  7. No swearing at the referee...they will send you off.
  8. If you consistently foul then you will get a yellow card which is 10 mins off the field
  9. If you do something dangerous such as a high tackle or tackle in the air you may get a red card which is completely sent off.
  10. Have fun and respect all in the game.
