ABOUT THE SITE

The reason this site exists is because my love of Rugby and my desire to share this with you with the hope of increasing the participation of rugby in the USA. You can go over the rules of the game to get a better understanding as well as find out about some famous players and their positions.If you are interested in playing there is a short form to fill out to see where you may play on the field. BE WARNED RUGBY IS NOT FOR THE FAINT HEARTED.So if the idea of tackling somebody or being tackled in the mud doesn't sound like fun to you this may not be your sport.
